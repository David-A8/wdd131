Tips before traveling

  • Plan and Organize: Research destination, create itinerary
  • Documentation: Check passport validity, obtain visas if needed
  • Health and Safety: Get vaccinations, consider travel insurance
  • Finances: Notify bank, carry local currency
  • Technology and Communication: Check phone plan, and verify that your company has coverage where you are going
  • Language and Culture: Learn basic phrases, respect local customs
  • Emergency Preparedness: Know emergency numbers, and share itinerary with friends or family

Packing tips

  • Make a Packing List: List essentials like clothes, toiletries, documents, and electronics
  • Check Weather and Destination: Pack accordingly for the weather and activities
  • Pack Light: Avoid overpacking; choose versatile clothing and roll clothes to save space
  • Use Compression Bags: Organize and compress clothes to maximize space
  • Pack Essentials in Carry-On: Include valuables, medications, and documents in case luggage is lost
  • Pack Toiletries Thoughtfully: Use travel-sized containers and pack in a clear, sealable bag
  • Consider Laundry Options: Bring detergent for longer trips or check for laundry facilities
  • Leave Room for Souvenirs: Plan space in your luggage for items you may buy during your trip
  • Double-Check: Ensure you have everything on your list, including travel documents

Safety Tips

  • Research Destination: Know local laws and customs
  • Secure Belongings: Use hotel safes and keep valuables secure
  • Stay Connected: Keep others informed of your plans
  • Beware of Scams: Stay vigilant in tourist areas and watch for pickpockets
  • Respect Local Customs: Dress and behave respectfully
  • Know Emergency Contacts: Have local emergency numbers handy
